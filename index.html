Financial Statement Preparation

The preparation of financial statements involves the process of aggregating accounting information into a standardized set of financials. Preparing financial statements; including the balance sheet, income statement, statement of retained earnings, and statement of cash flows; is the most important step in the accounting cycle because it represents the purpose of financial accounting.
